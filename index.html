About RCC

What We Do

RCC builds realistic roller coasters in Roblox using smooth track shaping, accurate pacing, and station systems inspired by real-world operations.

RobloxCFrameRealism

Design Philosophy

Focus on airtime, transitions, and ride flow. Every layout is tested for comfort, excitement, and visual impact.

Custom Systems

Dispatch logic, block zones, and train spacing designed from scratch for flexibility and performance.

Cedar Point-Style Inspiration

Layouts inspired by aggressive lift hills, fast transitions, strong airtime moments, and iconic station designs. The goal is to capture the feeling of riding coasters at the "Roller Coaster Capital" inside Roblox.
